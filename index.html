ABOUT ME

Researcher / Nara Institute of Science and Technology

Master Student of Engineering

Nara, Japan

Thank you for visiting my page!

I am studying spoken and natural language processing in Japan and I am deeply interested in how to handle self-supervised learning models of speech, in particular. Speech SSL models contain a wealth of internally useful information, and my research focuses on effectively extracting that information to improve the performance of downstream tasks.

I use PyTorch as my deep learning framework and I am skilled at implementing the logical components. Additionally, I have experience utilizing Python web development frameworks like Django and FastAPI, as well as proficiency in prototyping.
